39 /** Kernel used to quantize down the int32 accumulator values of GEMMLowp to QASYMM8/QASYMM8_SIGNED
40  *
41  * This kernel takes a final int32 accumulator value (the output of @ref CpuGemmLowpMatrixMultiplyKernel), and processes it to obtain the final QASYMM8/QASYMM8_SIGNED value.
42  * The following computations will be performed by the kernel:
43  *
44  * -# Add offset terms to final result
45  * -# Multiply each entry of result by result_mult_int
46  * -# Add bias to final result if bias tensor is not a nullptr
47  * -# Shift the int32 accumulator by result_shift
48  * -# Clamp the value between the specified min and max bounds
49  * -# Clamp the resulting int32 values:
50  * -# -to the [0..255] range and cast to QASYMM8.
51  * -# -to the [-128..127] range and cast to QASYMM8_SIGNED.
52  *
53  */
