35 /** Kernel to perform col2im reshaping.
36  *
37  * Rearranges each matrix column into image blocks. It's the inverse operation of @ref cpu::kernels::CpuIm2ColKernel.
38  *
39  * For example, a vector of 9 elements can be reshaped to a block(image) of 3x3:
40  *
41  * @f[
42  * \left( \begin{array}{ccccccccc}
43  * a0 & a1 & a2 & a3 & a4 & a5 & a6 & a7 & a8 \\
44  * \end{array} \right)
45  * \rightarrow
46  * \left( \begin{array}{ccc}
47  * a0 & a1 & a2 \\
48  * a3 & a4 & a5 \\
49  * a6 & a7 & a8 \\
50  * \end{array} \right)
51  * @f]
52  */
